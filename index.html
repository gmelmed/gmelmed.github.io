Featured work: Mapping rent changes in the US

Rent is rising in almost every county in the US, and in most counties it's outpacing inflation. Only a small handful of counties, mostly in the south, saw their rent index decrease over the past year. This map shows year-on-year change in US counties, which are sized by population. I recommend using the zoom and search features to explore the data.
