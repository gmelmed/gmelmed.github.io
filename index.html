Featured Work: New York's Linguistic Landscape

New York is an incredibly diverse city. Walking around the city, you're likely to hear dozens of languages every day. I used data from NYC Open Data to map to Map New York's linguistic landscape. This map shows the third most common language spoken in each community district. I map the third most common language because the first two are English and Spanish in almost every district.
